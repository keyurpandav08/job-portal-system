# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for everyone who participates in this project, regardless of experience level, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, personal appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, nationality, age, religion, or technical background.

This project exists to help people build real software that solves real problems. We expect all participants to contribute in a respectful, professional, and constructive manner.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that **contributes to a positive environment** include:

- Being respectful and considerate in communication  
- Providing constructive feedback and helpful suggestions  
- Using inclusive and welcoming language  
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences  
- Collaborating professionally in issues, pull requests, and discussions  
- Accepting responsibility for mistakes and learning from them  

Examples of **unacceptable behavior** include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or offensive comments  
- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory language  
- Trolling, spamming, or deliberately derailing discussions  
- Publishing private information without explicit permission  
- Intimidation or hostile behavior toward contributors or maintainers  

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including but not limited to:

- GitHub repositories  
- Issues and pull requests  
- Discussions and comments  
- Documentation and examples  
- Any communication related to this project  

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to violations of this Code of Conduct.

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

---

## Enforcement Guidelines

Consequences for unacceptable behavior may include:

1. **Warning** – A private or public reminder of expected behavior  
2. **Temporary Ban** – Limited participation in the project  
3. **Permanent Ban** – Removal from the community and project spaces  

The severity of the action will depend on the nature and impact of the violation.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it responsibly.

You may report concerns by:
- Opening a **private issue** (if supported), or  
- Contacting the repository owner or maintainers directly  

All reports will be reviewed confidentially and handled with care and respect.

---

## Community Expectations

This project is built for learning, collaboration, and real-world usage. Whether you are a beginner, student, professional developer, or contributor, your participation is valued as long as you follow these guidelines.

Be kind. Be professional. Build things that matter.
